Privacy & Data Policy
1. No Personal Data Collection

FaydaLink is a static directory. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information, Fayda ID numbers, or banking credentials. We do not have a database.

2. How it Works

This website functions solely as a collection of bookmarks. When you click on a service provider logo you are redirected away from our site to the official website of the service provider (e.g., Commercial Bank of Ethiopia). All data entry happens on their servers, not ours.

3. User Responsibility

While we verify links regularly, users are responsible for ensuring the URL in their browser address bar matches the official provider before entering sensitive info.

4. Hosting & Analytics

This site is hosted on Vercel. We may use anonymous, aggregate analytics (like Google Analytics) to see how many people visit the site, but this data cannot identify you personally.
